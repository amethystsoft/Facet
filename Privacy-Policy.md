## Privacy Policy for Amethyst Facet
Last Updated: June 29, 2026
This Privacy Policy governs your use of the Amethyst Facet application (the "Application"). The Application is designed as a zero-data-collection product.
## 1. Information Collection and Use

* No Personal Data: The Application does not collect any personally identifiable information. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or precise location.
* No Technical Data: The Application does not track or collect technical data, such as your hardware configuration, device identifiers, Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, or browser statistics.
* No Account Creation: You are not required to create an account or provide any credentials to use the full features of the Application.

## 2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

* No Cookies: The Application does not use cookies, tracking pixels, beacons, or local storage objects to monitor your behavior or log your data over time.
* No Third-Party Analytics: We do not integrate third-party frameworks or analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that monitor usage patterns or user behavior.

## 3. Data Storage

* Local Operation Only: Any settings, configurations, or processing you perform within the Application remain strictly on your local device.
* No Server Storage: We do not operate external database servers to store user logs or application inputs.

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

* No Data Selling: Because we do not collect any data, we cannot, do not, and will never sell, rent, trade, or share user data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties.
* Legal Compliance: We have no user data to provide to law enforcement, government bodies, or legal entities, even upon formal request.

## 5. Children's Privacy

* Full Safety: The Application does not knowingly or unknowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, in strict compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and global data regulations.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

* Policy Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted directly within this document with a revised "Last Updated" date. Because the core architecture of the Application is built on zero data collection, the fundamental nature of this policy will not change.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions, comments, or concerns regarding this zero-data Privacy Policy, you may reach out to the development team directly through the official distribution platform or store page.
